Meat Recepies

Mapo Tofu "麻婆豆腐"

Ingredients

Chili flakes / or Paprika for non spicy
Green Onions
Ground Garlic
Ground Ginger
Ground Pork/Chicken/ Beef / or all together you can even use vegan meat replacement
Soft Tofu (i can only find it in the asian supermarket)
Red Chili paste ( I use Gochujang)
Fermented black beans (black soy beans) you can use any black bean sauce
Sichuan Pepper ( use white peper if you dont have it )
Chicken stock or vegetable stock
Soy sauce
Chinese cooking wine
Olive oil / or Sunflower oil
Potato or Corn Starch

Directions

Prepare the tofu by carefully draining the water and slice in into cubes.

Start with toasting red chili flakes or the paprika with the oil until it turns red, add the ginger and the garlic stir and dont let it burn.

Add the white park of Green Onion then the ground meat, let it caramelize a little.

Meanwhile mixx all the liquid ingredients, one table spoon of soy sauce, the red bean paste( add to taste, depending on the level of spice), one tea spoon of whine, a tea spoon of corn starch , one table spoon of fermented beans and a sprinckle of pepper, mix it well.

When the meat is cooked add the chiken stock ( if you only have cubes, add it to water and microwave it for a few seconds so it can dissolve ) add to tast or level of salt, add the stock and water untill the meat is covered.

When its all coockes with the desired texture add the tofu and very very gently mix the tofu with the sauce, let it cook for 2 minutes while topping it with the green part of the Onions.

Serve it on top with a bowl of rice and you'll have a perfect meal.
