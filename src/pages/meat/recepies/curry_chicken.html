Chicken Curry "Frango com caril"

Ingredients

Curry powder that you like ( i tend to use one with less cumin)
(opcional for extra color) Turmeric
Olive oil and coconut oil
Fresh onion
garlic
ginger
Lime or lemon juice
chicken broth or vegetable broth
Canned tomatoes or fresh tomatoes
Chicken breasts
Cornstarch – used to thicken the sauce if you'd like
Coconut cream ( or milk) or Heavy cream
Chopped cilantro ( to top it off)
( Opcional ) Pinaple bits or Apple bits

Directions

Saute onion, garlic and ginger in a skillet with the olive oil or coconut oil, when golden Add broth and tomatoes, and the curry powder and turmric.

Let it cook for 5 minutes then add the coconut cream , the lime juice and the chicken bits, let it cook evenly and then add the pinaples if you want.

When i use apples i like to puree them first them add them to the sauce.

Add water when necessary, same goes for the starch.

Serve it with the fresh cilantro and basmati/ jasmin rice.

This recepie is very very simples, my mother and my godmother had different recepies for the same dish, they were both really good so i will post my recepie, a fusion from the two. Its not spicy so you can introduce this dish to kids, and let them get used to different spices.

Usually we use coconut milk and pinaple bits but you can use apple instead, its something i tried with my cousin and its also very very good. Maybe even easier for someone picky with their food.

You can even exclude all the fruits and still have a delicious creammy curry chicken.
