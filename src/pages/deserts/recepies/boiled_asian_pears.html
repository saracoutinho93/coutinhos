Desert Recepies

Boiled Asian Pears with Rock Sugar
"冰糖雪梨"

Ingredients

Asian Pears
Chinese Rock Candy
Goji Berries
Red dates
cinammon sticks
Anise Seeds
Chinese Cooking whine ( just a little bit )

Directions

Remove the cores of the pears then slice them the way you like.

Add pear and all the other ingredients into a pot.

Firstly cook on high heat. Turn the heat down to low when the water starts boiling.

Continue to cook for 15-20 minutes.
