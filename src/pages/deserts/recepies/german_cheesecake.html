Desert Recepies

German Cheesecake
"Käsekuchen"

Ingredients

250gr Flour
125 butter
40gr Sugar / honey
1 egg

Filling:

750 gr. Quark (fresh creamy cheese)
4 eggs (separate yolks and whites)
50 gr of flour
50 gr. sugar / honey
1/2 lemon- lemon zest
100gr raisins
50 gr melted butter

Directions

Add all the ingredients of the base and knead everything with your hands until you form a solid dough. Spread the base in a round shape with a removable opening. also cover the sides of the pan.

For the filling, add the Quark, egg yolks, sugar, lemon zest and flour and stir with an electric mixer. Add the butter and raisins.

Beat the egg whites and slowly incorporate into the filling.

Pour the filling into the pan and bake at 180 ° C for 45-50 minutes.

Remove from the oven and allow to cool. Pour out of the pan and serve cold.

So this is my Mother's recepie.
