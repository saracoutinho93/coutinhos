Desert Recepies

Aletria
"Aletria"

Ingredients

1.5 liter of water
250gr of vermicelli noodles ( aletria)
250gr of sugar
2 strips of lemon peel, only the yellow part
1 strip of orange peel
2 small cinnamon sticks
1 anise seed
2 egg yolks
powdered cinnamon to sprinkle

Directions

Place the water in the pan and bring to a boil along with the lemon peel, cinnamon stick, anise and sugar.

Bring to a boil, leave a little to get the flavor of cinnamon, the peels and the sugar is dissolved.

Add the vermicelli, let it cook for about 5 minutes, I don't like it very cooked so the cooking will suit everyone's taste.

While the vermicelli is cooking beat the egg yolks, remove some of the syrup from the mixture, mix in the egg yolks and stir well (to give a drop in temperature so that when placed directly they do not bake).
