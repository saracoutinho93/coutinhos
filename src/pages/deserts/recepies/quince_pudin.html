Desert Recepies

Quince Pudin
"Pudim de Marmelos"

Ingredients

150 g of quinces
5 eggs
3 gems
50 ml of milk
2 dl condensed milk
liquid caramel
lemon zest
ground ginger(optional)

Directions

Peel and pit the quinces with the lemon zezt and ginger and cook them in plenty of water .
Drain and puree them. Turn the oven to 180 Â° C.

Grease a rectangular shape with liquid caramel and set aside.
Mix the eggs, the yolks, milk, cooled quince puree and condensed milk very well. Pour into the pan and bake in a water bath in the middle of the oven for 50 minutes.

Let cool and serve decorated to taste.(a little cinammon is always nice).

Story Time

This is a Dad's recepie, we recently found out that the quinces change their taste ( bitterness) slightly deppending on the soil.

In Cerdeira, the Village where my grandparents lived we had a lot of Quince trees, the best i've ever tasted.

Dad found a recepie for a quince pudin, we never could have guess how good it was.

Because the recepie had no meaures, it took him many many tries to get it to his liking.

If you like true quince marmelade, you will love this.
