My Perfect Marinara Sauce
"Molho de tomate perfeito"

Ingredients

1 to 2 ripe tomato
fresh basil leaves
pepper
3 to 4 garlic cloves
1 onion (any kind will do)
parsely ( fresh or dried)
dried basil
garlic powder
chives freshly cut or dried
1 cube or beef/ vegetable / chicken stock , you can choose
half a bottle or one small can of tomato sauce or paste(use any kind , i tend to use one with either cheese or basil )
Olive oil
A pinch of sugar

Directions

In a blender or food processor add the sliced tomatoes, the garlic cloves , the onions the basil leaves and blend until a smooth paste is formed. Add a little bit of water if needed to ease the process.

In a pan heat the olive oil, when its hot add the paste and stir until its slightly cooked , add the stock cube or liquid stock and stir again, add the tomato sauce/ paste until you are pleased with the texture.

Let it cook for 15 minutes and then check the salt, if you need more add a pinch of it. Add half to one teaspoon of sugar and let it cook for 5 min. Keep in mind you can add water to your taste if you want the sauce less thick.
