Fish Recepies

Bread crust codfish "Bacalhau com Broa"

Ingredients

4 salted cod loins ( frozen or left to soak in water )
2 large thinly sliced onions
4 cloves of garlic
2 bay leaves
parsley
olive oil to tast

Corn bread crust:

corn bread
2 garlic cloves
bacon slices
parsley q. B.
salt and black pepper
a drizzle of olive oil

Directions

Chop all the ingredients for the corn crust (bread, garlic, parsley, bacon and olive oil).

Cook the cod for 5 minutes. Place a layer of onion with garlic, parsley, bay leaves and olive oil in the oven tray. Place the cod on top and sprinkle again with a little olive oil.

In a last layer, distribute the cornbread cover and water again with the remaining oil.

bake at 180ยบ for 30-40 minutes until the crust is golden in color.

Serve with punched potatoes and boiled turnip greens with garlc.
