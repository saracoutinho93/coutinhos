Fish Recepies

Backed Fish with Lemon "Peixe com limão à Judite"

Ingredients

2 to 3 sea bass ( you can use any fish )
2 lemons
2 onions
seasalt
pepper
olive oil
potatoes sliced into thick slices.
dried parsely

Directions

Season the fish with lemon juice, salta , dried parsely and pepper, leave two lemon slices inside the fish. You can add a lttle bit of water to help it cook.

In an oven tray lay a bed of sliced onions and sliced potatoes with olive oil and salt , be generous.

On top lay the fish and with a preheated over, let it cook for about 50min depe.

Turn the fish over once in a while to cook it properly, dont let the potatoes burn.
