Mussels stew "Caldeirada de Mexilh√£o "

Ingredients

1 kg of Mussels
1 large onion
1/2 green pepper / green pepper
100 ml of olive oil
150 ml of white wine
1 Large tomato well ripe
Piri-piri to taste
Salt to taste

Directions

Wash the mussels and discard those that are not closed properly.

In a large frying pan, bring the oil with the onion cut into quarters and the pepper cut into strips.

When the onion is transparent, add the tomato cut into pieces the piri-piri, season with salt and let it fry, until the tomato undoes, add the mussels.

Cover the pan and let them open over low heat.

When they are cooked, sprinkle with white wine.

Serve hot.
