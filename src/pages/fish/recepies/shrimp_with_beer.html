Shrimp with Beer "Camarão no forno com cerveja"

Ingredients

1Kg shrimp 20/30
salt to tast
1 lemon
butter
0.5 dl olive oil
1 Parsley
1 small Chilli
1 tea spoon of cayenne pepper
1.5 dl Beer

Directions

The day before preparing the meal, take the prawns out of the freezer and place them in the bottom of the refrigerator. In this way, they slowly defrost and maintain their natural flavor and texture.

Season with salt, lemon and let it taste for about 10 minutes.

Transfer to a tray with butter, olive oil, chopped garlic, chilli, allspice and beer.

Bake at 180ºC for approximately 15 minutes.

Remove from the oven and sprinkle with parsley.

Serve immediately.

Story Time

This dish can either be a full meal with bread or garlic toast or a starter, you decide, its a quick and easy recepie.

We use special chillies from africa that my dad buys from a friend.
