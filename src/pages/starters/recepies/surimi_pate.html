Starter Recepies

Surimi Paté "Paté de delicias"

Ingredients

Surimi, finely chopped
Mayonaise
Cocktail sauce
Garlic powder
Pinch of pepper
Parsely
Shredded Carrot
Corn (can)

Directions

Add all the dry ingredients into a mixing bowl, add mayo and a little bit of cocktail sauce and stir.

Season it to taste and Serve.

Story Time

This recepie is my own creation, so far i've never seen a combination like this, but like the tuna paté its a hit everytime.

I used to buy a sandwich with surimi paté when i was a teenager in subways kind of store. Because i didnt loike lettuce at the time i order it with corn and carrots, and the flavour combination was born.

This combinations is also perfect with chicken.

My mother always asks me to do the patés whenever we have guests.
