Spinach souflé "Souflé de espinafres da Mami"

Ingredients

1 small onion, chopped
500 gr. Spinach
50gr Butter
50gr Flour
6 eggs
30gr. grated Parmesan cheese
1 pinch of cayenne pepper
salt

Directions

Grease a soufle pan with butter.

Coock the spinach leaves in boiling water for approximately 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and finely chop them.

Separate the yolks from the whites. Beat the egg whites into hard peaks.

Melt the butter and sauté the onion for 5 minutes. Add the flour, salt and pepper and let it golden brown. Add the milk, stirring constantly and let the stew thicken. Mix the egg yolks in the stew over low heat, stirring quickly.

Add the chopped spinach to the preparation and mix everything.

Finally, slowly incorporate the beaten egg whites with a silicone spatula. Pour into the buttered pan.

Bake at 180º for 40 minutes.
