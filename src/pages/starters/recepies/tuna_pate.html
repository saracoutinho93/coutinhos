Starter Recepies

Tuna Paté "Paté de atum"

Ingredients

1 to 2 tuna cans
Mayonaise
Cocktail sauce
Bellpepers ( whatever colour you want)
Garlic powder
Pinch of peper
Parsely

Directions

Drain the oil from the tuna, and the palce the fish nto a mixing bowl.

With a fork sligthly grind/mix the meat , after it seperates add the peppers sliced into cubes, the parsely garlic,pepper and mix.

Lastly add mayo and a bit of cocktail sauce until you get and even textures like the pictures.

Enjoy with toasts/ bread or crackers.

Story Time

I first tried this paté in Guarda, my friends father was cook and i learned this recepie from him, the secret is the bellpeppers, it gives the paté a fresh bite.

Originally it also had raw onions, but since im not a huge fan i dont add them. So far its been a hit with all our guests.
