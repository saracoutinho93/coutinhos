Chestnut Soup "Sopa de castanhas"

Ingredients

Water
Peeled chestnuts ( i use the frozen ones, they are raw )
Celary
Sweet potato ( optional)
Carrots
Chicken or vegetable broth
Shreded shicken or bakon bits ( optional)
Heavy cream
Chives
Onion or french onion ( leak )
Garlic powder
Pepper to taste

Directions

Add everything but the cream into a pot and let it cook for 30 to 60 minutes, add water to cover it fully , and add more if necessary.

Once is cooked and you are pleased with the level of salt use your blender or hand blender until smooth, add the cream and let it cook for a few minutes.

Serve it with bacon beets or shreded chicken, i usually serve it with a drizle or cream and the chives.

Story Time

The first time i tried chestnut soup it was a cold german day, i was invited to dinner.

The restaurante had this soup, and I feel in love, i tried to recreate the flavour i tasted, im very pleased with the result, its pure comfort food and we love it.
